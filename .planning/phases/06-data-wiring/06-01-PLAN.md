`startRefreshJobs()` must:

1. **Eager startup fetch** — Before scheduling any crons, call prices, news, and sanctions fetchers immediately (wrapped in try/catch, log errors but don't crash). This prevents the status bar from showing "offline" on first start (Pitfall 5 from research).

2. **Prices cron** — `cron.schedule('0 */6 * * *', ...)` (every 6 hours). Alpha Vantage free tier is 25 req/day; 6-hour interval = 8 req/day. Inside the cron: call `fetchOilPrices()`, iterate results and call `insertPrice()` for each. Log `[CRON] Prices refreshed: N symbols` on success. Log error on failure but don't crash.

3. **News cron** — `cron.schedule('*/30 * * * *', ...)` (every 30 min, 48 req/day within NewsAPI 100/day limit). Call `fetchNews()`, iterate and call `insertNewsItem()`. Log `[CRON] News refreshed: N headlines`.

4. **Sanctions cron** — `cron.schedule('0 2 * * *', ...)` (daily at 2 AM). Call `fetchSanctionsList()`, iterate and call `upsertSanction()`. Log `[CRON] Sanctions refreshed: N entries`.

5. Log startup summary (same style as detection-jobs.ts): `Refresh cron jobs scheduled: prices every 6h, news every 30m, sanctions daily`.

Write test scaffold in `refresh-jobs.test.ts` using Vitest. Mock all external imports with `vi.mock(...)`.
